Ingredients:
- 1 piece of smoked jam
- 1 piece of cheddar
- 2 pieces of white bread
- Some butter

Directions:
1.Put the jam and the chesse in the middle of the white bread
2.Put some butter in the sides of the white bread without the chesse and jam
3.Put the sandwich in the pan for one minute at full power
4.Turn the sandich in the pan to heat the other side and wait for 1 minute
5.Put the sandwhich in a small plate. Ready to serve
